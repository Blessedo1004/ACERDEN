About Us

Our Chairman/CEO

Prof. Daniel Sunday Ugwu is a Professor/Reader in the Department of Agricultural Economics & Extension, Enugu State University of Science & Technology (ESUT). He is an Agricultural Economist by profession as well as a Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E) Specialist.

Prof. Ugwu was the Ag. Programme Manager (1999 - 2000) and Director Planning, Monitoring & Evaluation (PME) (1991 - 2002) of a World Bank-Assisted Project, the Enugu State Agricultural Development Programme. He also served as the President of a Civil Society Organisation, the Enugu State Coalition of Civil Societies on HIV/AIDS (ESCCHA) (2003-2009).

Qualification-wise, Prof. Ugwu, holds a Ph. D (Agricultural Economics) (2000) , M. Sc. (Agricultural Economics) (1986) and B. Agric. (Animal Science) (1982).
